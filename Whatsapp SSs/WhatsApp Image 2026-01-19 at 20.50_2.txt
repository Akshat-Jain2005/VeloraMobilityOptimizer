The flag infeasible_handling_required indicates that a test case may contain infeasible scenarios, but teams must identify and demonstrate infeasibility through their own analysis, rather than assuming it by default.
Teams should not simply declare the case infeasible; instead, they must find the best possible solution by relaxing secondary
constraints (such as sharing or vehicle preferences, but not exceeding maximum delay limits), optimise overall cost, and clearly report
which constraints were relaxed, why the original problem was infeasible, and how the final solution was derived, noting that metadata is provided as guidance and strict adherence is not mandatory but deviations must be justified.
